"Adopt, Don't Shop"

This was a web and video project about animal adoption close to Texas State University. Both of my dogs were adopted from Humane Society of New Braunfels. I wanted to do a project not only on a non-profit, but on something I felt passionate about.

"Company Theatre"

I occasionally work as a Production Stage Manager for City Theatre Company in Austin, TX. My first inspiration to study web design and development was from realizing that many small theatres and troupes do not have the funds and staff to dedicate to maintaining a strong website. I developed this one by thinking of ways to improve upon my home company's page.

"Art Near You"

This web project was inspired by an idea that someone new to Austin might be looking for various types of art venues. From music, performing art, and museums, this would be a one stop shop to search for venues in the area.

"Extracurricular in Texas"

I developed this web project based on financial data for Texas public schools in Region 13 (Central Texas). I conducted research with data from the previous five years to compare funding allocated for extracurricular activities to funding allocated for athletics.
