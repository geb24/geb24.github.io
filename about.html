About

Hello

My name is Gwendolyn Balboa. I am studying Digital Media and Mass Communication at Texas State University. I aspire to work in a career in Arts Administration. I have been learning a little bit of everything from web design and development, video editing, and publication design. This portfolio is a sample of some of my projects.

I moved to San Marcos, Texas after graduating with my Associate of Arts degree from Victoria College. I graduated Summa Cum Laude with my Bachelor of Fine Arts in Theatre from Texas State University after studying abroad in Stratford-upon-Avon and London. My concentration was in Performance and Production with an emphasis in Stage Management. While in London, I had the opportunity to go behind the scenes with a Deputy Stage Manager at the Royal Shakespeare Company. It really inspired me to continue working in Theatre after returning home.
